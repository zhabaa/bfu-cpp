The Kukeri Festival The Kukeri Festival is one of the oldest traditions in Bulgaria. It happens every year in winter. Men wear special costumes and wear big, scary masks that look like animals. The men dance and make loud noises with bells. They do this to scare away bad spirits and bring good luck for the new year. The Kukeri Festival is very colourful and exciting.
